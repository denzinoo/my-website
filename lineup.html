Artist Lineup

Here are some of the artists and bands featured at MuiscFest 2025. The brief description give attendees an idea of the of music each one performs

Andy Taylor

Hip-Hop

Energetic Hip-Hop with strong beats and quick verses. Brings unique hip-hop style to the festival.

Rustwire

Rock

Loud, more intense music with drums and electric guitars. Gets the crowd very lively and energetic.

DJ Kwamez

Aforbeats

Plays mix of African type beats with upbeat rhythms. Good music for all people from African/Carribean backrounds.

Neon Bloom

Electro Pop

Mixes pop vocals with electronic sounds. Most songs are upbeat creating intense atmosphere.

The Rasta Waves

Reggae

Warm guitar rhythms, steady drum patterns and positve, feel good lyrics. Creates an easy atmosphere for the crowd to enjoy.
