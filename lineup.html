2025 Artist Lineup

Here are some of the artists and bands featured at MuiscFest 2025. The brief description give attendees an idea of the of music each one performs

Main Stage Artists

Drake

Hip-Hop / R&B

Drake is a hip-hop and R&B artist known for catchy hooks and big anthems. His set includes a mix of rap verses and melodic singing.

Burna Boy

Afro Beats

Burna Boy brings Afro Beats and afro-fusion sounds to the festival. His music mixes African rhythms with modern beats, creating a lively and dancaable atmosphere.

Adele

Pop / Soul

Adele is a pop and soul singer with powerful vocals. Her set includes emotional ballards and slower songs that give the festival a more relaxed moment.

Gunna

Hip Hop / Trap

Gunna performs hip-hop and trap music with heavy bass and laid back flows. His performance is aimed at fans of mordern rap.

Rema

Afro beats

Rema adds more Afro Beats to the lineup with upbeat songs and catchy choruses. His tunes is good for dancing and crowd particpation.
